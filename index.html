About me

Who is Nina?

I am an international student pursuing a degree in Computer Science and Economics. I was born in Moscow, attended a boarding school in New York, and now finishing my senior year of college (through Zoom) in Los Angeles. Leaving home at the age of 15 taught me independence, adaptability, and the importance of taking risks. With my double major degree, I am planning to pursue a career in the fintech space.


In my free time, I enjoy drinking a cup of strong black coffee, running, taking ballet classes, or blasting music and trying to sing along (my talent is to mess up the lyrics).
